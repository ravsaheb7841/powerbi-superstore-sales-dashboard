# 📊 Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

A modern, interactive **Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard** built using the **Sample Superstore Dataset**. This project provides an executive-level overview of sales performance, customer behavior, regional trends, and product profitability through intuitive visualizations and actionable business insights.

> Designed as a portfolio-ready dashboard with a professional dark theme, interactive navigation, KPI cards, and data-driven insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The dashboard transforms raw Superstore sales data into meaningful business insights that help stakeholders monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed decisions.

The report is divided into four analytical pages:

- Executive Overview
- Product & Regional Performance
- Product Analysis
- Customer Overview

---

# ✨ Dashboard Features

### 📈 Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of overall business performance.

**KPIs**
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value

**Visuals**
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by State (Map)
- Sales by Category
- Top Products by Sales
- Interactive Filters
- Key Business Insights

---

### 🌍 Product & Regional Performance

Analyzes regional sales contribution and category-wise profitability.

**KPIs**
- Most Profitable Category
- Lowest Profit Category
- Highest Sales Region
- Highest Sales Contribution

**Visuals**
- Sales by Region & Category
- Profit Heatmap
- Interactive Filters
- Business Insights

---

### 📦 Product Analysis

Identifies top-performing and loss-making products.

**Visuals**
- Top 10 Products by Profit

- Profit Distribution by Category

**KPIs**
- Total Profit from Top 10 Products
- Total Loss from Bottom 10 Products
- Highest Product Profit
- Total Products Analyzed

**Business Insights**
- Revenue Leak Identification
- Product Performance Analysis
- Optimization Recommendations

# 📈 Key Business Insights

- Technology is the highest-performing category in terms of profitability.
- California and the West region contribute significantly to overall sales.
- Several products generate high sales but negative profit, indicating pricing and discount optimization opportunities.
- The Consumer segment represents the largest customer base.
- A high repeat purchase rate indicates strong customer loyalty.
